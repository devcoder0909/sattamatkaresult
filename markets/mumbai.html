Market Overview

The Mumbai market is a significant part of the Matka ecosystem. Known for its specific schedule and distinct chart patterns, it attracts a large following of dedicated researchers who analyze its historical data to understand numerical probability.

Chart Analysis

Analyzing the Mumbai charts (both Jodi and Panel) involves looking at the historical results recorded over weeks and months. By studying these cyclical records, one can observe frequency distributions of specific panels and Jodi combinations.
